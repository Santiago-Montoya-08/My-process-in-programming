About Colombia

Colombia is a country located in South America. It has wonderful landscapes and beautiful beaches. Also, the country has very polite people.

  • Manizales
  • Pereira

Colombia's top touristic places

Embalse de Guatapé in Guatapé, Antioquia

  • Located in Antioquia
  • Known for its beautiful reservoir

Catedral de las Lajas in Pasto, Nariño

  • Located in Nariño
  • Gothic Revival style basilica church

Caño Cristales

  • Located in Meta department
  • Also known as the most beautiful river in the world

Sierra Nevada de Santa Marta in Santa Marta, Magdalena

  • Located in Magdalena
  • Highest coastal mountain range in the world

Isla de los Micos in Amazonas

  • Located in the Amazon
  • Known for its large population of monkeys
